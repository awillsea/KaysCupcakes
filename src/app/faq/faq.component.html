FAQ

Common questions

Here are some of the most common questions that we got.

Do you offer gluten-free options?

Yes, we offer a variety of gluten free desserts. Please specify your dietary restrictions when placing a custom order

Can i place a custom order for a specific occasion?

Absolutely! We love creating custom desserts for specials occasions. Simply visit the "Custom Orders" section and fill out the form with your requirements

How far in advance should i place a custom order?

We recommend placing custom orders at least 1 week in advance to ensure availability. However, feel free to contact us for any last-minute request and we will do our best to accommodate.

Do you deliver?

Yes, we offer delivery services within a 10-mile radius. Delivery charges may apply based on the distance. Please provide your address during the checkout process to see if you are eligible for delivery

What payment methods do you accept?

we accept all major credit cards, including Visa, Mastercard, and American Express. Cash on delivery is also available for local orders
